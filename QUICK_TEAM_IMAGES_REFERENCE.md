## 📸 Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Size**: 300x300px (minimum, preferably square)
- **File Size**: < 500KB recommended
- **Quality**: Professional headshots

## 🛠️ How Images Are Displayed

1. **Image Found**: Shows actual image with zoom effect on hover
2. **Image Missing**: Shows auto-generated placeholder with member name
3. **Load Error**: Automatically falls back to placeholder
